Please read me

Some of the conversations today could touch on intimacy, the body or vulnerable things.

If at any moment you feel uncomfortable, that is completely okay. Just know you are never obligated to share, listening is also contributing.

Main Activity

The 4 stations

Each station has a fixed host and one conversation card. You stay 10 minutes, then rotate clockwise when the bell rings.

1
Arrive at the station
The host welcomes your group and introduces the card prompt.
2
Conversation begins
Discuss, respond, question. Share as much or as little as you like.
3
Leave a trace
The host writes down key words or ideas your group found meaningful — building a collective map.
4
Bell rings → rotate ↻
Move clockwise to the next station. After 4 rounds you've visited them all.

Roles

Who does what

Most of you will be participants. Here's what each role means.

🚶
Participant
You move with your group from station to station. You listen, contribute, and help leave a trace of the conversation.
📜
Station Host
Stays at the same station the whole time. Welcomes each new group, introduces the card, and listens carefully, acting as the memory keeper by noting key words and ideas on the paper.
🔔
Facilitator
Moves across the whole space, supports hosts and participants, keeps time, and rings the bell to signal each rotation.

At each station

The host
is a volunteer

Each station is guided by one of you — a volunteer who stays in place for the whole activity and holds the conversation for every group that passes through.

As a host, you will
  • → Welcome each new group arriving at your station
  • → Read the card prompt aloud and open the conversation
  • → Listen for key words, images and ideas — write them down on the paper
  • → By the end, your sheet becomes a collective map of everything said at your station
🏺
At every station you'll find an altar
Each station has a small altar set up with markers and paper. Use them freely — to write, draw, or leave any trace of your conversation.
